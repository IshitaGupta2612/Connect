The Challenge in Tech

Despite national conversations about gender diversity in tech, women and non-binary are still underrepresented, underpaid, and often discriminated against in the tech industry, numbers show. This is true for BIPOC aswell. There are many organisations aiming to bridge the gender gap but organising mentorship opportunities but they all said connectivity and automation was an barrier for optimised mentorship

What our application provides?
  • Twilio powered easy communication without need to share personal details
  • Algorithm for automated pairing of mentors and mentees
  • Opportunities for marginalised genders
  • Profile Flashcards to connect to any mentor/mentee for support

Inspiration Corner

Let's get inspired by women in technology and learn from their stories to get empowered.

Ada is referred to as the first programmer because she had written notes that explained how the notion of a specific engine could transition calculation to computation. She is one of the famous women in technology and every second Tuesday in October is known as Ada Lovelace Day to celebrate the achievements of women in STEM careers.

Ada Lovelace

Reshma Saujani

Ellen K. Pao

Frequently Asked Questions

You have got any questions for us? Check out the frequently asked questions? Incase you don't find your answers you can ping us at: connectcause@gmail.com

  • What is connect and what do we aim for?

    Connect is a one stop support for organisations which aim to bridge the gender gap in technology.It provides a platform for easy automated mentorship cycle/connectivity pairing, provides opportunities and connects indivisuals to communities that can inspire them ans support them. We aim to provide marginalised genders access to opporunities and a right fit community to keep them motivated. We have numerous partner organisations. One of our biggest aims is to enhance connectivity.

  • How can I get paired or get involved?

    If you are willing to be paired up with an industry expert or are willing to guide someone in their tech jouney scroll further down to fill up the form for a mentor or mentee. If you are a organisation in tech supporting marginalised genders shoot us an email now. Incase you have any opportunties you would like to present or have any ideas or suggestions please email us aswell.

  • Is this for free?

    Yes, this website and all the organisations who we have partnered with are non-profit organisations which are free and have a sole purpose of making the world a better place :)

  • How can this space help you?

    If you are a marginalised gender in tech, seeking a safe community and free access to opportunities or an organisation owner willing to spread good, this is the right place for you

  • How are we different?

    We cater to the problems and support non profit organisations are facing and provide a one stop solution to them aswell as ease connectivity
